SocaLabs effects plug-in installer
==================================

This installer places a single SocaLabs effects plug-in into your system audio
plug-in folders.

Components:
  • VST                  /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST
  • VST3                 /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3
  • Audio Unit           /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components
  • CLAP                 /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/CLAP

Plugins that bundle factory presets install them under
  /Library/Audio/Presets/SocaLabs/<PluginName>

User-saved presets live under
  ~/Library/Audio/Presets/SocaLabs/<PluginName>
and are not touched by this installer.
